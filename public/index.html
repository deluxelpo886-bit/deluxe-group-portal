Alerts & Notifications

The system automatically checks for expiring LPOs and overdue generator services every 60 seconds, and re-alerts on anything still critical once every 24 hours.

Sound alarm on new critical alert
Desktop/browser notification
Auto-open WhatsApp on critical alert

If your alerts go to a WhatsApp Group: WhatsApp does not let any website open a specific group chat or auto-send into it - that's a WhatsApp/Meta restriction, not something this app can bypass. Use "Copy Alert Message" below, then paste it into your group yourself. The "Send WhatsApp" button only works for an individual contact number (set above in General Settings), where it pre-fills the message but you still tap Send once. Note also that most browsers block pop-ups that aren't triggered by a direct click - "Auto-open WhatsApp" may get silently blocked when it fires from the background alarm check rather than a button press; if that happens you'll see a warning toast telling you to use "Copy Alert Message" instead.
